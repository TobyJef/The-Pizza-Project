The Pizza Project

Slice to meet you

Here at the Pizza Project, our aim is to bring together the best locally sourced ingredients and freshly made pizza straight to you and your community, via our bespoke pizza truck.
Everything from our hand stretched pizza dough through to our sauce and ingredients are prepared on the day, while the pizza's are assembled and cooked on site, ensuring that we can deliver the freshest slice around.

We are available for private hire, corporate functions and charity/community events. For availability and what you KNEAD to know, head over to our booking page.
We believe in the power of local community and that charity starts at home. So that is why we donate 5% of our booking fee to local charities within the community. Or we will waive our booking fee if we are used for charitable events.
